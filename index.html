About Svalbard

Svalbard is a remote Arctic archipelago located halfway between mainland Norway and the North Pole. It's one of the northernmost inhabited places on Earth where polar bears roam, the Northern Lights dance, and the sun never sets in summer.

Its untouched landscapes and surreal light make it a rare place that reminds us how vast and delicate our planet truly is.
